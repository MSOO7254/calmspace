# CalmSpace — Product Roadmap & Vision

## What CalmSpace Is
A personalized emotional companion platform that adapts to who you are.
Same app, different soul — depending on the user.

---

## Core Concept — Personality System

### Six Voice Tones
1. 🌸 Soft & Cute — gentle, pastel, emoji-heavy, lowercase
2. 🔥 Blunt & Real — raw, no sugar-coating, "okay babe, get up" energy
3. 🖤 Edgy / Hacker — dark default, monospace, terse, "system: mood logged"
4. ☁️ Chill & Poetic — slow, reflective, journal-like, soft metaphors
5. ⚡ Hype & Motivational — high energy, caps-lock moments, "LET'S GOOO"
6. 🌙 Warm & Nostalgic — cozy, comforting, late-night friend energy

### Personality Sliders (set during onboarding quiz)
- Introvert ↔ Extrovert
- Chill ↔ High Energy
- Cute/Soft ↔ Edgy/Raw
- Structured ↔ Free-flow
- Private ↔ Open to matching with others

### What Changes Based on Personality
- AI companion reply style after each mood check-in
- Color theme (mint-soft, dark-hacker, sunset-warm, etc.)
- Font feel (rounded Nunito vs monospace vs serif-journal)
- Suggested activities (solo journaling vs group match-up)
- How the mood ballot reads ("how's your heart? 🌿" vs "status report:")

## Age Groups

### Children (with parental controls)
- Parent creates account and manages child's profile
- Simple, playful interface with big buttons and characters
- Mood check-ins through games and colors not words
- Integration with games children love
- Parent dashboard to monitor child's emotional patterns
- No personal data collected from children directly

### Gen Z
- Gaming culture integration (Call of Duty, PUBG, FIFA vibes)
- Fast, visual, meme-aware tone
- Gamification — streaks, badges, rewards
- Customized wallpapers based on personality

### Millennials
- Warm nostalgic tone
- Journaling features
- Work-life balance focused moods
- Longer reflective responses

### Gen Alpha
- Highly visual and interactive
- Short attention span friendly
- Character companions
- Voice interaction potential

### Older Users
- Larger text options
- Simpler navigation
- Warm and calm tone only
- Family connection features

## Visual Theming System

### Personality Based Themes
- 🌸 Soft — pastel pinks, mints, rounded fonts, floral touches
- 🖤 Hacker — dark background, green monospace text, terminal aesthetic
- ☁️ Calm — soft grays, serif fonts, lots of white space
- ⚡ Hype — bold colors, strong typography, high contrast
- 🌙 Nostalgic — warm creams, soft oranges, cozy textures
- 🔥 Raw — stark black and white, bold sans-serif, no fluff

### Customized Wallpapers
- Generated based on personality quiz results
- Categories: hacker, calm, nature, abstract, cozy, energetic
- User can also pick manually
- Changes with mood if user enables it

## Gamification
